Chunky Chicken Quesadilla

SERVES 2

PREP TIME 5 MINUTES

COOKING TIME 15 MINUTES

315 CALORIES

30 GRAMS PROTEIN

28 GRAMS CARBOHYDRATES

9 GRAMS FAT

  • 1 (6-ounce) boneless, skinless chicken breast, trimmed of fat
  • 1 tablespoon low-fat sour cream
  • 2 (8-inch) whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1/3 cup salsa
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/3 cup shredded low-fat cheddar cheese
  1. Coat a medium nonstick skillet with cooking spray and warm over medium heat. Add chicken and cook for 3 to 5 minutes per side. Once fully cooked, transfer chicken to a cutting board.
  2. Spread sour cream onto 1 tortilla. Slice chicken breast and layer it over sour cream, topping with salsa and lettuce. Sprinkle with cheese and top with the other tortilla.
  3. Recoat skillet with cooking spray and warm over low heat. Cook quesadilla until golden, about 3 minutes per side, using a large spatula to carefully flip it. Remove from the skillet, slice, and serve
